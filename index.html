Featured Project

Linchpin Coffee

Developed out of a love for coffee, a desire to push myself, and to learn new things comes the site for Linchpin Coffee. Though a fictional coffee company, Linchin is full of personality! Coded using HTML, PHP & SASS and also integrated as a custom WordPress theme, the site features a full screen scrolling parallax on the home page, a brew guide, blog, and so much more!

One of my absolute favorite pages is the brew guide! The simplicity of it all combined with the details of giving the user an experience where they can truly follow along step by step and brew great coffee at home using the exact methods their favorite coffee shop is using. The page is laid out with flexbox is an alternating pattern with images and text being apread out across from one another. When the learn more button is clicked is when the magic happens. A modal windw appears with instructions on the equipment needed and step by step instructions to brew their favorite type of coffee.

I love this site! Linchpin Coffee brought its own set of challenges and major opportunited for growth that have made me a better developer all-around. From learning and using PHP for the very first time to then taking the site and fully integrating it into WordPress as a custom developed theme allowing a client the opportunity to update anything they needed to on their own.
